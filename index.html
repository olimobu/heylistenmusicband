Video game, Movie & Anime Music Cover Band

About

Cover band formed by María García (piano), Bego Ramos (violin) and Oliver Moya (clarinet), born in Málaga (Spain) with the aim of spreading video game, movie and anime music.

Since our first concert in 2017, we have played in a multitude of events throughout the geography of Malaga (Polo Digital for Glovalues and the University of Malaga, White and Black Night for the City Council of Malaga, El Corte Inglés, Marbecon, RinconGo, AlhaurínGo and Level Up!), as well as outside of it (FicZone + Granada Gaming Festival, LucenaGo and MarchenaGo).
